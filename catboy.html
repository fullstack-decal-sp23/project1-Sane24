The Catboys FanArt Corner

On this page, you'll find information about some of my favorite catboys and their fanart.

Catboys in Anime / Manhwa / Games

Nagi Seishiro (Blue Lock)

Nagi Seishiro is a talented soccer player and the captain of the Blue Lock team. He's known for his cool and collected demeanor, but he also has a softer side that he doesn't show to many people.

Chongyun (Genshin Impact)

Chongyun is a member of the Liyue faction and a skilled ice user. He's a bit of a goofball and can be a bit clumsy at times, but he's fiercely loyal to his friends and will do whatever it takes to protect them.

Karma Akabane (Assassination Classroom)

Karma Akabane is a student in Class 3-E of Kunugigaoka Middle School and one of the top assassins in the class. He's known for his quick wit and clever mind, but he also has a bit of a sadistic streak and enjoys causing chaos.

Kim Dokja (Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint)

Kim Dokja is the main character of Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint. He's a smart and resourceful individual with the ability to read the future, and he uses his skills to try to change the course of events and save the world.
